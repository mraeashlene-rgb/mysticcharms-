About Us

Mystic Charms started as a school project and grew into a small business inspired by creativity, softness, and self-expression. Each piece is made to match your mood and style.

What began as experimenting with beads, tiny charms, and different styles grew into pieces designed for mixing, matching, and stacking with ease. Each piece is thoughtfully made to add a subtle touch of charm and personality to your everyday look.

From a classroom project to a growing small business, Mystic Charms is all about self-expression — your style, your mood, your rules.

Frequently Asked Questions

Do you accept returns or offer refunds? +

We currently do not offer a return policy due to the nature of our products, as most items are handmade and customized based on the customer's request. However, if your order arrives damaged, you may contact us within 48 hours and provide clear photo proof. We will gladly offer a replacement or a refund depending on the situation.

How long does order processing and delivery take? +

Orders are usually processed and delivered within 2–3 days, depending on the quantity of items purchased. For bulk orders exceeding five items, processing and delivery may take 3–5 days.

Are the crystals authentic? +

Our products are not made from high-end or premium materials, but we make sure that they are of good quality and worth their price range of ₱75–₱80.

Can I customize my order? +

Yes, customization is available. You may contact us through our social media accounts and send your preferred design or idea. We will do our best to create a product based on your request.

What payment methods do you accept? +

We accept online payments through GCash by scanning our QR code. We also offer Cash on Delivery (COD) for customers who prefer to pay upon receiving their order.

Can I cancel my order? +

Orders can only be canceled before they are processed. Once the item is already being prepared or shipped, cancellation is no longer allowed.

Do you offer gift wrapping? +

Yes, we offer gift wrapping for an additional fee. You may message us in advance if you want this service. We can also include a short personalized message for your gift.

Where are you located? +

We are based in Daet, Camarines Norte, and orders are shipped from our location.

Can I track my order? +

Yes, you can track your order by checking the "My Orders" section on our website. Updates regarding your order status will be shown there.

What makes your charms special? +

Our charms are designed to be simple, meaningful, and affordable. Each piece is carefully made with attention to detail to ensure both quality and aesthetic appeal, making them a great choice for personal use or as gifts.
